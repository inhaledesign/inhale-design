PWP Milestone 1

Overview

Purpose
To share my development projects in a way that reflects the unique geometric user experiences in those projects.
Audience
The primary audience will be people involved in hiring for development and development-adjacent positions. This includes recruiters, hiring managers, CEOs, CTOs, and professional developers.
The secondary audience will be other developers to whom I would like to demonstrate my work and share development related information with.
Goals
I am going to obtain full time employment as a developer in either the public or private sector. My PWP will be a gallery and professional profile that I submit with my resume.

Persona: Harry McHires, Tech Recruiter

Background
Harry is a recruiter for a tech company that develops its own B2C products. His job involves screening candidates' applications, posting job listings, and searching for potential candidates' websites and social media.
He is technically savvy, having some professional work developing before moving into the recruiting position.
He's really into paintball and trying to get traction with his fledgling consulting business.
Loves
Harry likes when his day job is easy. He wants to save his time and energy for working on his business and paintball.
Hates
Harry needs to recommend viable candidates for interviews, but with the increase of applicants using LLMs to write their resumes, it's hard to separate the wheat from the chaff. This means he has to work harder and has been struggling to focus on his other activities.
Technology used
MacBook Air running macOS 13 Ventura, browses with Safari 16.6
iPhone 14 running iOS 17, browses with Safari 16.6

Scenario

Setup
Harry McHires has reviewed my resume and is looking to review my actual work. He's opened his browser on his MacBook and is looking at the front page of my site.
Goal
Harry has a lot of applicants. He's thrown out the obvious rejects, and now he's sifting through his pile of applicants for ones that stand out as likely hires.
Story
As a tech recruiter, I need to quickly weigh if the person on the site should be interviewed for the position they've applied to.

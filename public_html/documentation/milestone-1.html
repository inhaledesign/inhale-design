PWP Milestone 1

Overview

Purpose
To share my development projects in a way that reflects the unique geometric user experiences I have designed.
Audience
The primary audience will be people involved in hiring for development and development-adjacent work. This includes recruiters, hiring managers, CEOs, CTOs, and professional developers.
The secondary audience will be other developers to whom I would like to demonstrate my work and share development related information with.
Goal
I am going to obtain full time employment as a developer in either the public or private sector. My PWP will be a gallery and professional profiles that I submit with my resume. I will also use it in networking with other developers.

Persona: Harry McHires, Professional Dev Recruiter

Harry is a recruiter for a tech company that develops its own products for market. His job involves screening candidates' applications, posting job listings, and searching for potential candidates on the web and social media.
He is technically savvy, having some professional work developing before moving into the recruiting position.
He's really into paintball and trying to launch his own consulting business. He tries to do his hiring manager job as efficiently as possible to make sure he has time and energy for his outside activities.

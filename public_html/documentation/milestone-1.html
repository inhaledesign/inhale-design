PWP Milestone 1

Overview

Purpose
The purpose of my PWP is to share my development projects in a way that reflects the unique geometric user experiences I have designed.
Audience
The primary audience will be people involved in hiring for development and development-adjacent work. This includes recruiters, hiring managers, CEOs, CTOs, and professional developers. The secondary audience will be other developers to whom I would like to demonstrate my work and share development related information with.
Goal
I am going to obtain full time employment as a developer in either the public or private sector. My PWP will be a gallery and professional profiles that I submit with my resume. I will also use it in networking with other developers.
